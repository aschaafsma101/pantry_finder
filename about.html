About Us

Meal Makers is a team dedicated to reducing food insecurity and supporting students at BMCC and the surrounding community. Our mission is to make nutritious food more accessible and affordable for everyone. Inspired by the goal of “Zero Hunger,” we’ve developed a platform to help students find meal options that fit their needs and budget.

What We Offer

Our website connects students to BMCC’s Panther Pantry and other local food resources. Users can view real-time inventory updates from the Panther Pantry, sign up for pantry services online, and access affordable meal plans based on dietary needs and preferences. We also provide links to other food pantries and soup kitchens in NYC, making it easier to find nearby resources.

Our Vision

We’re committed to creating a supportive and hunger-free community. With more funding, we hope to expand our services, adding features like premium access to high-demand items and nutritional guidance. Meal Makers is here to help students focus on their education without worrying about their next meal.
